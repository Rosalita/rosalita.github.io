My name is Rosie and I work as a Software Developer, this is my blog.

I am passionate about developing with Go, collaborating with other people and sharing experiences.

I have worked within software teams for a _long_ time. I started out as a Games Tester in 2005 then moved on to testing business software in 2014. By 2017 I had a number of talks accepted and started speaking internationally at large software testing conferences. At the start of 2018 I accepted my first job as a Software Developer.

It's a tough, but it's also made me tough. I know this is what I want to do with my life, I like proving people wrong and succeeding against difficult odds. I am grateful to be part of an amazing community of Go developers and for all the friends I have made along the way.

I also have a testing blog, where I used to write quite a bit about testing software.
